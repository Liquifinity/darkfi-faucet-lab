# Code of Conduct

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and technically specific.
- Critique ideas, code, and evidence rather than people.
- Keep security discussions responsible and non-exploitative.
- Do not pressure maintainers or contributors to expose private wallets, keys, infrastructure, or personal systems.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks.
- Publishing private keys, wallet artifacts, personal paths, credentials, or exploit details.
- Social engineering contributors for access to wallets, nodes, Railway, GitHub, or local machines.
- Submitting changes intended to exfiltrate secrets, weaken controls, or mislead users.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may close issues, delete comments, block users, or revoke access when behavior creates security or community risk.

Security-sensitive reports should follow [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md).
